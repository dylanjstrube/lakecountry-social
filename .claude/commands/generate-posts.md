## Content Rules

**Headlines — make people stop scrolling:**
- Hook on pain (splinters, maintenance, embarrassment, wasted weekends) OR aspiration (dream deck, entertaining, neighborhood pride)
- Use seasonal context: spring → summer prep / urgency; summer → "right now"; fall → plan ahead; winter → dream for next year
- No brand taglines, no company name, no hashtags in the headline
- ≤8 words, punchy, direct

**Captions:**
- Conversational, warm, "neighbor who's a deck expert" tone — never salesy
- Reference Wisconsin / Lake Country at least once
- End with the CTA (website or phone)

**CTA distribution (per 10-post batch):**
- 8 posts → website: `www.lakecountrydecking.com`
- 2 posts → phone: `(920) 355-2174`

## 10 Required Angles (one per post, no repeats)

Use each of these exactly once per batch to ensure diversity:

1. **Pain-point** — splintering boards, annual maintenance grind, dangerous bare feet, embarrassment hosting
2. **Seasonal urgency** — get the deck ready before the season fills up, crews booking fast
3. **Aspirational** — dream outdoor space, sunset cocktails, summer entertaining you've always wanted
4. **Neighborhood pride** — best deck on the block, neighbors will notice
5. **Financial/value** — 25–50 year warranty, never spend on maintenance again, ROI
6. **Time liberation** — no more staining, sealing, or sanding — ever. Reclaim your summer weekends.
7. **Transformation** — dramatic before/after, one week of work, completely different home

9. **Holiday/entertaining** — tie to upcoming occasion (Memorial Day, 4th of July, summer BBQ season)
10. **FOMO** — your neighbors already did it, here's why everyone in Lake Country is resurfacing this season
